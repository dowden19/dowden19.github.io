About Me

 I am currently a Junior at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science and Quantitative Biology. I am studying Computer Science because I enjoy tackling challenging problems and the feeling of satisfaction when my code finally works. Additionally, I like how programming can be applied to solve problems in every field. I am also studying Biology because I love learning about how our bodies work on a molecular level; I just finished probably my favorite class of all time which was Developmental Biology. I found it fascinating how we develop from single celled zygotes into fully functioning organisms. My goal is to find a career that combines my background in both, such as using machine learning to analyze biological data. However, in the meantime, I want to explore both fields, as I know they are highly dynamic and there is so much I have yet to learn.

 For fun, I like playing Ultimate Frisbee on the club team at UNC, playing games with friends, reading novels, thrifting clothes and searching for little statues to put in my plants.
